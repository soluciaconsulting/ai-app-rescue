Frequently asked questions

Do you need access to my code?
Usually, yes, but only what's needed. Most AI builders let you export your project or invite a collaborator. We'll sort out the safest way to get me what I need on our consultation call.
How much does it cost?
Every app is different, so I quote a fixed price after the free 15-minute consult. You'll know the exact cost before any work starts, with no hourly surprises.
How long does a fix take?
Small fixes can turn around in a few days. Larger rescues such as rebuilding auth, redesigning a database, or hardening for production typically run one to two weeks. I'll give you a timeline with your quote.
I'm not technical. Is that a problem?
Not at all. Most of my clients aren't. I'll explain what's wrong and what I'm doing in plain English, and you'll end up with an app you can actually understand and grow.
Can you keep building features after the fix?
Absolutely. Many founders keep me on as a technical partner once their app is stable, whether that's new features, ongoing maintenance, or fractional CTO support.
What makes you different from another AI tool?
I'm a real software engineer with 10+ years of experience building enterprise software across healthcare, insurance, and e-commerce. AI tools get you to a prototype; I make sure it's secure, fast, and ready for real users.
